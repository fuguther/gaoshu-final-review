(7)\(\displaystyle \iint\limits_{S} \boldsymbol{F}\cdot\boldsymbol{n}dS, 其中 \boldsymbol{F}=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}\{x,y,z\}, S 是区域 1\leq x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}\leq 2 的边界曲面的外侧\)
设 \(r=\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}\)，则 \(\boldsymbol{F}=r\{x,y,z\}=\{rx,ry,rz\}\)
计算 \(\frac{\partial(rx)}{\partial x}=\frac{x}{r}\cdot x+r=\frac{x^2}{r}+r=\frac{x^2+r^2}{r}=\frac{x^2+x^2+y^2+z^2}{r}=\frac{2x^2+y^2+z^2}{r}\)
由轮换对称性，\(\frac{\partial(ry)}{\partial y}=\frac{2y^2+x^2+z^2}{r}\)，\(\frac{\partial(rz)}{\partial z}=\frac{2z^2+x^2+y^2}{r}\)

由高斯公式，\(\iint\limits_{S}\boldsymbol{F}\cdot\boldsymbol{n}dS=\iiint\limits_{\Omega}\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{F}dV=\iiint\limits_{\Omega}4\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}dV\)
其中 \(\Omega: 1\leq x^2+y^2+z^2\leq 2\)，采用球坐标：\(x=\rho\sin\varphi\cos\theta, y=\rho\sin\varphi\sin\theta, z=\rho\cos\varphi\)，\(dV=\rho^2\sin\varphi d\rho d\varphi d\theta\)
积分区域为 \(1\leq\rho\leq\sqrt{2}\)，\(0\leq\varphi\leq\pi\)，\(0\leq\theta\leq 2\pi\)
\[\iiint\limits_{\Omega}4\rho\cdot\rho^2\sin\varphi d\rho d\varphi d\theta=4\int_0^{2\pi}d\theta\int_0^{\pi}\sin\varphi d\varphi\int_1^{\sqrt{2}}\rho^3 d\rho\]
\[=4\cdot 2\pi\cdot\left[-\cos\varphi\right]_0^{\pi}\cdot\left[\frac{\rho^4}{4}\right]_1^{\sqrt{2}}\]
\[=8\pi\cdot(1+1)\cdot\frac{1}{4}\left((\sqrt{2})^4-1^4\right)\]
\[=16\pi\cdot\frac{1}{4}\cdot(4-1)\]
\[=4\pi\cdot 3\]
答案\(12\pi\)
