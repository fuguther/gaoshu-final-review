1.求通解

(1)\(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1+y^{2}}{(1+x^{2})xy}\)
\[\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{1+y^{2}}{(1+x^{2})xy}\]
\[\frac{y}{1+y^{2}}dy=\frac{1}{x(1+x^{2})}dx\]
\[\frac{y}{1+y^{2}}dy=\left(\frac{1}{x}-\frac{x}{1+x^{2}}\right)dx\]
\[\int\frac{y}{1+y^{2}}dy=\int\frac{1}{x}dx-\int\frac{x}{1+x^{2}}dx\]
\[\frac{1}{2}\ln(1+y^{2})=\ln|x|-\frac{1}{2}\ln(1+x^{2})+C_{1}\]
\[\frac{1}{2}\ln(1+y^{2})=\ln|x|-\frac{1}{2}\ln(1+x^{2})+\frac{1}{2}\ln C\]
\[\ln(1+y^{2})=2\ln|x|-\ln(1+x^{2})+\ln C\]
\[\ln(1+y^{2})=\ln\frac{Cx^{2}}{1+x^{2}}\]
\[1+y^{2}=\frac{Cx^{2}}{1+x^{2}}\]
\[y^{2}=\frac{Cx^{2}}{1+x^{2}}-1=\frac{Cx^{2}-1-x^{2}}{1+x^{2}}=\frac{(C-1)x^{2}-1}{1+x^{2}}\]
令 \(C_{2}=C-1\)，则 \(y^{2}=\frac{C_{2}x^{2}-1}{1+x^{2}}\)
\[y=\pm\sqrt{\frac{C_{2}x^{2}-1}{1+x^{2}}}\]
答案\[y=\pm\sqrt{\frac{Cx^{2}-1}{1+x^{2}}}\]（其中 \(C\) 为任意常数，且需满足 \(Cx^{2}>1\) 使根号有意义）
(2)\(\displaystyle a\left(x\frac{dy}{dx}+2y\right)=xy\frac{dy}{dx}\)
原方程为 \(a\left(x\frac{dy}{dx}+2y\right)=xy\frac{dy}{dx}\)
展开得 \(ax\frac{dy}{dx}+2ay=xy\frac{dy}{dx}\)
移项整理：\(ax\frac{dy}{dx}-xy\frac{dy}{dx}=-2ay\)
提取公因式：\(x(a-y)\frac{dy}{dx}=-2ay\)
分离变量：\(\frac{a-y}{y}dy=-\frac{2a}{x}dx\)
左边拆项：\(\left(\frac{a}{y}-1\right)dy=-\frac{2a}{x}dx\)
两边积分：\(\int\left(\frac{a}{y}-1\right)dy=\int-\frac{2a}{x}dx\)
计算积分：\(a\ln|y|-y=-2a\ln|x|+C_1\)
整理：\(a\ln|y|+2a\ln|x|=y+C_1\)
即 \(a\ln|x^2y|=y+C_1\)
指数化：\(|x^2y|=e^{\frac{y+C_1}{a}}\)
去掉绝对值：\(x^2y=Ce^{\frac{y}{a}}\)，其中 \(C=\pm e^{\frac{C_1}{a}}\) 为任意非零常数
当 \(C=0\) 时，\(y=0\) 也是原方程的解（代入原方程验证：左边 \(=a(0+0)=0\)，右边 \(=0\)）
故通解为 \(x^2y=Ce^{\frac{y}{a}}\)，\(C\) 为任意常数
答案\(x^2y=Ce^{\frac{y}{a}}\)（\(C\) 为任意常数）
(3)\(\displaystyle y-xy'=a(y^{2}+y')\)
原方程 \(y-xy'=a(y^2+y')\)
整理得 \(y-ay^2=xy'+ay'=(x+a)y'\)
即 \((x+a)\frac{dy}{dx}=y-ay^2=y(1-ay)\)
分离变量：\(\frac{dx}{x+a}=\frac{dy}{y(1-ay)}\)
对右边作部分分式：\(\frac{1}{y(1-ay)}=\frac{A}{y}+\frac{B}{1-ay}\)
\(1=A(1-ay)+By\)，令 \(y=0\) 得 \(A=1\)；令 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\) 得 \(B=a\)
故 \(\frac{1}{y(1-ay)}=\frac{1}{y}+\frac{a}{1-ay}\)
积分两边：\(\int\frac{dx}{x+a}=\int\left(\frac{1}{y}+\frac{a}{1-ay}\right)dy\)
\(\ln|x+a|=\ln|y|-\ln|1-ay|+C_1=\ln\left|\frac{y}{1-ay}\right|+C_1\)
\(\ln|x+a|=\ln\left|\frac{y}{1-ay}\right|+C_1\)
\(|x+a|=e^{C_1}\left|\frac{y}{1-ay}\right|\)，即 \(x+a=C\frac{y}{1-ay}\)（\(C=\pm e^{C_1}\)为任意非零常数，\(C=0\)亦包含特解）
\((x+a)(1-ay)=Cy\)
\(x+a-ay(x+a)=Cy\)
\(x+a=Cy+ay(x+a)=y(C+a(x+a))\)
由 \(x+a=C\frac{y}{1-ay}\) 解出 \(y\)：\((x+a)(1-ay)=Cy\)
\(x+a-ay(x+a)=Cy\)
\(x+a=y(C+ax+a^2)\)
\(y=\frac{x+a}{C+ax+a^2}\)
令 \(C\) 为任意常数，验证 \(y=0\) 时原方程左边\(=0\)，右边\(=a(0+y')=ay'\)，得\(y'=0\)，即\(y=0\)为常数解，已含于通解中（取\(C\to\infty\)）
另验证 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\)：左边\(=\frac{1}{a}-x\cdot 0=\frac{1}{a}\)，右边\(=a(\frac{1}{a^2}+0)=\frac{1}{a}\)，是特解；但 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\) 时 \(1-ay=0\)，分离变量失效，需检验是否含于通解：若 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\)，则 \(x+a=C\frac{1/a}{0}\) 无有限 \(C\)，故 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\) 为额外特解
答案通解为 \(y=\frac{x+a}{C+ax+a^2}\)（\(C\) 为任意常数），另有特解 \(y=\frac{1}{a}\)

(8)\(\displaystyle (x+2y)dx+(2x-3y)dy=0\)
设 \(M=x+2y\)，\(N=2x-3y\)
计算 \(\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}=2\)，\(\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}=2\)

(9)\(\displaystyle (3x+5y)dx+(4x+6y)dy=0\)
\( (3x+5y)dx+(4x+6y)dy=0 \)
验证：\( P=3x+5y \)，\( Q=4x+6y \)
\( \frac{\partial P}{\partial y}=5 \)，\( \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}=4 \)
因 \( \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \neq \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} \)，非全微分方程
求积分因子：\( \frac{1}{Q}\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}\right)=\frac{5-4}{4x+6y}=\frac{1}{4x+6y} \) 非仅 \( x \) 函数
\( \frac{1}{P}\left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\right)=\frac{4-5}{3x+5y}=\frac{-1}{3x+5y} \) 非仅 \( y \) 函数
尝试齐次方程：令 \( y=ux \)，\( dy=udx+xdu \)
\( (3x+5ux)dx+(4x+6ux)(udx+xdu)=0 \)
\( x(3+5u)dx+x(4+6u)(udx+xdu)=0 \)
\( (3+5u)dx+(4+6u)(udx+xdu)=0 \)
\( (3+5u+4u+6u^2)dx+(4+6u)xdu=0 \)
\( (3+9u+6u^2)dx+(4+6u)xdu=0 \)
\( (3+9u+6u^2)dx=-(4+6u)xdu \)
\( \frac{dx}{x}=-\frac{4+6u}{3+9u+6u^2}du \)
\( 3+9u+6u^2=3(1+3u+2u^2)=3(1+u)(1+2u) \)
\( \frac{4+6u}{3(1+u)(1+2u)}=\frac{A}{1+u}+\frac{B}{1+2u} \)
\( 4+6u=3A(1+2u)+3B(1+u) \)
\( u=-1 \)：\( 4-6=3A(-1) \)，\( -2=-3A \)，\( A=\frac{2}{3} \)
\( u=-\frac{1}{2} \)：\( 4-3=3B\cdot\frac{1}{2} \)，\( 1=\frac{3B}{2} \)，\( B=\frac{2}{3} \)
\( \frac{4+6u}{3(1+u)(1+2u)}=\frac{2}{3(1+u)}+\frac{2}{3(1+2u)} \)
\( \frac{dx}{x}=-\left(\frac{2}{3(1+u)}+\frac{2}{3(1+2u)}\right)du \)
\( \ln|x|=-\frac{2}{3}\ln|1+u|-\frac{1}{3}\ln|1+2u|+C_1 \)
\( 3\ln|x|=-2\ln|1+u|-\ln|1+2u|+C_2 \)
\( \ln|x^3|+\ln|(1+u)^2|+\ln|1+2u|=C_2 \)
\( \ln|x^3(1+u)^2(1+2u)|=C_2 \)
\( x^3(1+u)^2(1+2u)=C \)
代回 \( u=\frac{y}{x} \)
\( x^3\left(1+\frac{y}{x}\right)^2\left(1+\frac{2y}{x}\right)=C \)
\( x^3\cdot\frac{(x+y)^2}{x^2}\cdot\frac{x+2y}{x}=C \)
\( (x+y)^2(x+2y)=C \)
答案\( (x+y)^2(x+2y)=C \)

(11)\(\displaystyle 2(x+y)dx+ydy=0\)
方程为 \(2(x+y)dx+ydy=0\)，即 \(\frac{dy}{dx}=-\frac{2(x+y)}{y}\)
令 \(u=\frac{y}{x}\)，则 \(y=ux\)，\(dy=udx+xdu\)
代入得 \(2(x+ux)dx+ux(udx+xdu)=0\)
整理得 \(2x(1+u)dx+u^2xdx+ux^2du=0\)
即 \(x[2(1+u)+u^2]dx+ux^2du=0\)
分离变量得 \(\frac{dx}{x}+\frac{u}{u^2+2u+2}du=0\)
对分母配方：\(u^2+2u+2=(u+1)^2+1\)
改写分子：\(u=(u+1)-1\)
积分得 \(\int\frac{dx}{x}+\int\frac{(u+1)-1}{(u+1)^2+1}du=C\)
即 \(\ln|x|+\int\frac{u+1}{(u+1)^2+1}du-\int\frac{1}{(u+1)^2+1}du=C\)
计算得 \(\ln|x|+\frac{1}{2}\ln[(u+1)^2+1]-\arctan(u+1)=C\)
代回 \(u=\frac{y}{x}\)：\(\ln|x|+\frac{1}{2}\ln\left[\left(\frac{y}{x}+1\right)^2+1\right]-\arctan\left(\frac{y}{x}+1\right)=C\)
化简对数项：\(\left(\frac{y+x}{x}\right)^2+1=\frac{(x+y)^2+x^2}{x^2}=\frac{x^2+2xy+y^2+x^2}{x^2}=\frac{2x^2+2xy+y^2}{x^2}\)
因此 \(\ln|x|+\frac{1}{2}\ln\frac{2x^2+2xy+y^2}{x^2}-\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}=C\)
即 \(\ln|x|+\frac{1}{2}\ln(2x^2+2xy+y^2)-\ln|x|-\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}=C\)
化简得 \(\frac{1}{2}\ln(2x^2+2xy+y^2)-\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}=C\)
或写成 \(\ln(2x^2+2xy+y^2)-2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}=C_1\)
进一步整理：\(\ln(2x^2+2xy+y^2)=2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}+C_1\)
即 \(2x^2+2xy+y^2=e^{C_1}\cdot e^{2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}}\)
令 \(C_2=e^{C_1}>0\)，则 \(2x^2+2xy+y^2=C_2e^{2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}}\)
也可写成 \(2x^2+2xy+y^2=Ce^{2\arctan\left(1+\frac{y}{x}\right)}\)，其中 \(C\) 为任意常数
答案\(2x^2+2xy+y^2=Ce^{2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}}\) 或等价地 \((x+y)^2+x^2=Ce^{2\arctan\frac{x+y}{x}}\)
(12)\(\displaystyle 2xdz-2zdx=\sqrt{x^{2}+4z^{2}}dx\ (x>0)\)

令 \(z=xu\)，则 \(\frac{dz}{dx}=u+x\frac{du}{dx}\)
代入得 \[2x\left(u+x\frac{du}{dx}\right)-2xu=\sqrt{x^2+4x^2u^2}\]
化简左边：\[2xu+2x^2\frac{du}{dx}-2xu=2x^2\frac{du}{dx}\]
化简右边：\[\sqrt{x^2(1+4u^2)}=x\sqrt{1+4u^2}\]（因 \(x>0\)）
方程化为 \[2x^2\frac{du}{dx}=x\sqrt{1+4u^2}\]
分离变量：\[\frac{2}{\sqrt{1+4u^2}}du=\frac{dx}{x}\]
左边积分：\[\int\frac{2}{\sqrt{1+4u^2}}du=\int\frac{2}{\sqrt{1+(2u)^2}}du\]
令 \(t=2u\)，\(dt=2du\)，积分变为 \[\int\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+t^2}}dt=\ln|t+\sqrt{1+t^2}|=\ln|2u+\sqrt{1+4u^2}|\]
右边积分：\[\int\frac{dx}{x}=\ln|x|=\ln x\]（因 \(x>0\)）
得 \[\ln(2u+\sqrt{1+4u^2})=\ln x+\ln C=\ln(Cx)\]
即 \[2u+\sqrt{1+4u^2}=Cx\]
回代 \(u=\frac{z}{x}\)：\[\frac{2z}{x}+\sqrt{1+\frac{4z^2}{x^2}}=Cx\]
即 \[\frac{2z}{x}+\frac{\sqrt{x^2+4z^2}}{x}=Cx\]
两边乘 \(x\)：\[2z+\sqrt{x^2+4z^2}=Cx^2\]
移项：\[\sqrt{x^2+4z^2}=Cx^2-2z\]
两边平方：\[x^2+4z^2=C^2x^4-4Cx^2z+4z^2\]
消去 \(4z^2\)：\[x^2=C^2x^4-4Cx^2z\]
除以 \(x^2\)（\(x>0\)）：\[1=C^2x^2-4Cz\]
解出 \(z\)：\[4Cz=C^2x^2-1\]
\[z=\frac{C^2x^2-1}{4C}=\frac{Cx^2}{4}-\frac{1}{4C}\]
令 \(C_1=\frac{C}{2}\)，则 \(C=2C_1\)，代入得 \[z=\frac{2C_1\cdot x^2}{4}-\frac{1}{4\cdot 2C_1}=\frac{C_1x^2}{2}-\frac{1}{8C_1}\]
或直接令常数为 \(C\)，写为 \[z=\frac{Cx^2}{2}-\frac{1}{4C}\]
答案\[z=\frac{Cx^2}{2}-\frac{1}{4C}\] 或等价地 \[z=\frac{C_1}{2}x^2-\frac{1}{4C_1}\]，其中 \(C\)（或 \(C_1\)）为任意正常数（保证 \(Cx^2-2z>0\) 使平方根有意义）

设 \(M = 2x^2 + y^2\)，\(N = 2xy + 3y^2\)

(42)\(\displaystyle (x^{2}+y^{2}+2x)dx+2ydy=0\)
观察方程 \((x^{2}+y^{2}+2x)\mathrm{d}x+2y\mathrm{d}y=0\)，改写为 \(2y\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}=-(x^{2}+y^{2}+2x)\)
令 \(u=y^{2}\)，则 \(\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d}x}=2y\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}\)，方程化为 \(\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{d}x}+u=-(x^{2}+2x)\)
这是一阶线性方程，积分因子 \(\mu(x)=e^{\int 1\mathrm{d}x}=e^{x}\)
两边乘 \(e^{x}\)：\(\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}(ue^{x})=-(x^{2}+2x)e^{x}\)
计算 \(\int x^{2}e^{x}\mathrm{d}x=x^{2}e^{x}-2\int xe^{x}\mathrm{d}x=x^{2}e^{x}-2(xe^{x}-e^{x})=(x^{2}-2x+2)e^{x}\)
计算 \(\int 2xe^{x}\mathrm{d}x=2(xe^{x}-e^{x})=(2x-2)e^{x}\)
故 \(\int(x^{2}+2x)e^{x}\mathrm{d}x=(x^{2}-2x+2)e^{x}+(2x-2)e^{x}=x^{2}e^{x}\)
因此 \(ue^{x}=-x^{2}e^{x}+C\)
即 \(y^{2}e^{x}=-x^{2}e^{x}+C\)
整理得 \((x^{2}+y^{2})e^{x}=C\)
答案\((x^{2}+y^{2})e^{x}=C\)
(43)\(\displaystyle \frac{y}{x}dx+(y^{3}-\ln x)dy=0\)
设 \(M=\frac{y}{x}\)，\(N=y^{3}-\ln x\)
计算 \(\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}=\frac{1}{x}\)，\(\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}=-\frac{1}{x}\)
\(\frac{\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}}{N}=\frac{\frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{x}}{y^{3}-\ln x}=\frac{\frac{2}{x}}{y^{3}-\ln x}\) 仅与 \(y\) 有关不成立，改求 \(\frac{\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}}{M}=\frac{-\frac{1}{x}-\frac{1}{x}}{\frac{y}{x}}=\frac{-\frac{2}{x}}{\frac{y}{x}}=-\frac{2}{y}\)
积分因子 \(\mu(y)=e^{\int-\frac{2}{y}dy}=e^{-2\ln|y|}=\frac{1}{y^{2}}\)（取 \(y>0\)）
乘以积分因子得 \(\frac{1}{xy}dx+\left(y-\frac{\ln x}{y^{2}}\right)dy=0\)
验证新方程：\(\tilde{M}=\frac{1}{xy}\)，\(\tilde{N}=y-\frac{\ln x}{y^{2}}\)，\(\frac{\partial\tilde{M}}{\partial y}=-\frac{1}{xy^{2}}\)，\(\frac{\partial\tilde{N}}{\partial x}=-\frac{1}{xy^{2}}\)
求 \(u(x,y)\) 使 \(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{xy}\)
\(u=\int\frac{1}{xy}dx=\frac{\ln x}{y}+\varphi(y)\)
由 \(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=-\frac{\ln x}{y^{2}}+\varphi'(y)=y-\frac{\ln x}{y^{2}}\)
得 \(\varphi'(y)=y\)，\(\varphi(y)=\frac{y^{2}}{2}\)
故 \(u=\frac{\ln x}{y}+\frac{y^{2}}{2}\)
通解为 \(\frac{\ln x}{y}+\frac{y^{2}}{2}=C\)
答案\(\frac{\ln x}{y}+\frac{y^{2}}{2}=C\) 或等价地写为 \(2\ln x+y^{3}=Cy\)（\(C\) 为任意常数）
(44)\(\displaystyle (x\cos y-y\sin y)dy+(x\sin y+y\cos y)dx=0\)
设 \(M=x\sin y+y\cos y\)，\(N=x\cos y-y\sin y\)
计算偏导数，\(\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}=x\cos y+\cos y-y\sin y\)，\(\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}=\cos y\)
计算 \(\frac{\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}}{N}=\frac{x\cos y+\cos y-y\sin y-\cos y}{x\cos y-y\sin y}=\frac{x\cos y-y\sin y}{x\cos y-y\sin y}=1\)
积分因子 \(\mu(x)=e^{\int 1\,dx}=e^x\)
方程两边乘 \(e^x\)：\(e^x(x\sin y+y\cos y)\,dx+e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)\,dy=0\)
验证恰当性：\(\frac{\partial}{\partial y}[e^x(x\sin y+y\cos y)]=e^x(x\cos y+\cos y-y\sin y)\)
\(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}[e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)]=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)+e^x\cos y=e^x(x\cos y+\cos y-y\sin y)\)
恰当，求原函数 \(u(x,y)\)
\(u=\int e^x(x\sin y+y\cos y)\,dx\)，对 \(x\) 积分，视 \(y\) 为常数
分部积分：\(\int xe^x\sin y\,dx=\sin y\cdot(xe^x-e^x)=e^x(x-1)\sin y\)
\(\int ye^x\cos y\,dx=ye^x\cos y\)
故 \(u=e^x(x-1)\sin y+ye^x\cos y+\varphi(y)=e^x(x\sin y-\sin y+y\cos y)+\varphi(y)\)
对 \(y\) 求偏导：\(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=e^x(x\cos y-\cos y+\cos y-y\sin y)+\varphi'(y)=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)+\varphi'(y)\)
与 \(N\) 比较：\(e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)+\varphi'(y)=e^x(x\cos y-y\sin y)\)
得 \(\varphi'(y)=0\)，即 \(\varphi(y)=C_0\)
取 \(u=e^x(x\sin y+y\cos y-\sin y)=C_1\)
整理：\(e^x[(x-1)\sin y+y\cos y]=C\)
答案\(e^x[(x-1)\sin y+y\cos y]=C\)

(3)\(\displaystyle xdy-ydx=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}dx,\ 当\ x=\frac{1}{2}\ 时,\ y=0\)
方程化为 \(xdy - ydx = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}dx\)，即 \(xdy = (y + \sqrt{x^2+y^2})dx\)
当 \(x = \frac{1}{2} > 0\)，设 \(y = ux\)，则 \(dy = udx + xdu\)
代入得 \(x(udx + xdu) = (ux + \sqrt{x^2+u^2x^2})dx = (ux + |x|\sqrt{1+u^2})dx\)
因 \(x > 0\)，有 \(|x| = x\)，故 \(x(udx + xdu) = x(u + \sqrt{1+u^2})dx\)
除以 \(x\) 得 \(udx + xdu = (u + \sqrt{1+u^2})dx\)
化简得 \(xdu = \sqrt{1+u^2}dx\)
分离变量 \(\frac{du}{\sqrt{1+u^2}} = \frac{dx}{x}\)
积分两边 \(\int\frac{du}{\sqrt{1+u^2}} = \int\frac{dx}{x}\)
得 \(\ln(u + \sqrt{1+u^2}) = \ln|x| + C_1\)
即 \(u + \sqrt{1+u^2} = Cx\)（其中 \(C = e^{C_1}\)，且因 \(x > 0\) 取正）
由 \(u = \frac{y}{x}\) 代回，得 \(\frac{y}{x} + \sqrt{1+\frac{y^2}{x^2}} = Cx\)
即 \(\frac{y + \sqrt{x^2+y^2}}{x} = Cx\)，故 \(y + \sqrt{x^2+y^2} = Cx^2\)
代入初始条件 \(x = \frac{1}{2}, y = 0\)：\(0 + \sqrt{\frac{1}{4}+0} = C \cdot \frac{1}{4}\)
即 \(\frac{1}{2} = \frac{C}{4}\)，解得 \(C = 2\)
特解满足 \(y + \sqrt{x^2+y^2} = 2x^2\)
移项 \(\sqrt{x^2+y^2} = 2x^2 - y\)

化简 \(x^2 = 4x^4 - 4x^2y\)
因 \(x \neq 0\)，除以 \(x^2\) 得 \(1 = 4x^2 - 4y\)
解得 \(y = x^2 - \frac{1}{4}\)
验证：当 \(x = \frac{1}{2}\) 时，\(y = \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{4} = 0\)，符合初始条件；且 \(\sqrt{x^2+y^2} = 2x^2 - y = 2x^2 - x^2 + \frac{1}{4} = x^2 + \frac{1}{4} \geq 0\)，满足非负要求
答案\(y = x^2 - \frac{1}{4}\)

(5)\(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{2y}{x}=x^{2}e^{x},\ 当\ x=1\ 时,\ y=0\)
方程为 \(\frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{2}{x}y=x^{2}e^{x}\)，这是一阶线性微分方程，标准形式为 \(\frac{dy}{dx}+P(x)y=Q(x)\)，其中 \(P(x)=-\frac{2}{x}\)，\(Q(x)=x^{2}e^{x}\)
积分因子 \(\mu(x)=e^{\int P(x)dx}=e^{\int -\frac{2}{x}dx}=e^{-2\ln|x|}=e^{\ln x^{-2}}=\frac{1}{x^{2}}\)
方程两边乘以积分因子：\(\frac{1}{x^{2}}\frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{2}{x^{3}}y=e^{x}\)
左边为 \(\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{y}{x^{2}}\right)=e^{x}\)
两边积分：\(\frac{y}{x^{2}}=\int e^{x}dx=e^{x}+C\)
通解为 \(y=x^{2}(e^{x}+C)=x^{2}e^{x}+Cx^{2}\)
代入初始条件 \(x=1\)，\(y=0\)：\(0=1^{2}\cdot e^{1}+C\cdot 1^{2}=e+C\)
解得 \(C=-e\)
特解为 \(y=x^{2}e^{x}-ex^{2}=x^{2}(e^{x}-e)\)
答案\(y=x^{2}(e^{x}-e)\)
(6)\(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}+\frac{2y}{x}=\frac{1}{x^{2}},\ 当\ x=1\ 时,\ y=2\)
方程为 \(\frac{dy}{dx}+\frac{2}{x}y=\frac{1}{x^{2}}\)，其中 \(P(x)=\frac{2}{x}\)，\(Q(x)=\frac{1}{x^{2}}\)
积分因子 \(\mu(x)=e^{\int P(x)dx}=e^{\int \frac{2}{x}dx}=e^{2\ln|x|}=e^{\ln x^{2}}=x^{2}\)（取 \(x>0\)）
方程两边乘以 \(x^{2}\)：\(x^{2}\frac{dy}{dx}+2xy=1\)
左边为 \(\frac{d}{dx}(x^{2}y)=1\)
积分得 \(x^{2}y=\int 1\,dx=x+C\)
通解为 \(y=\frac{x+C}{x^{2}}=\frac{1}{x}+\frac{C}{x^{2}}\)
代入初始条件 \(x=1\)，\(y=2\)：\(2=\frac{1}{1}+\frac{C}{1}=1+C\)
解得 \(C=1\)
特解为 \(y=\frac{1}{x}+\frac{1}{x^{2}}=\frac{x+1}{x^{2}}\)
答案\(y=\frac{x+1}{x^{2}}\)
(7)\(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}+y\tan x=\sec x,\ 当\ x=0\ 时,\ y=-1\)
识别一阶线性方程标准形式 \(\frac{dy}{dx} + P(x)y = Q(x)\)，得 \(P(x) = \tan x\)，\(Q(x) = \sec x\)
计算积分因子 \(\mu(x) = e^{\int \tan x \, dx} = e^{-\ln|\cos x|} = \frac{1}{\cos x} = \sec x\)
方程两边乘积分因子：\(\sec x \frac{dy}{dx} + y \sec x \tan x = \sec^2 x\)
左边化为全微分：\(\frac{d}{dx}(y \sec x) = \sec^2 x\)
两边积分：\(y \sec x = \int \sec^2 x \, dx = \tan x + C\)
解出通解：\(y = \tan x \cdot \cos x + C \cos x = \sin x + C \cos x\)
代入初始条件 \(x = 0\)，\(y = -1\)：\(-1 = \sin 0 + C \cos 0 = 0 + C \cdot 1\)
得 \(C = -1\)
答案\(y = \sin x - \cos x\)
(8)\(\displaystyle \frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{2y}{x+1}=(x+1)^{3},\ 当\ x=0\ 时,\ y=1\)
方程为 \(\frac{dy}{dx}-\frac{2y}{x+1}=(x+1)^{3}\)，这是一阶线性方程，标准形式为 \(\frac{dy}{dx}+P(x)y=Q(x)\)
其中 \(P(x)=-\frac{2}{x+1}\)，\(Q(x)=(x+1)^{3}\)
积分因子 \(\mu(x)=e^{\int P(x)dx}=e^{\int -\frac{2}{x+1}dx}=e^{-2\ln|x+1|}=e^{\ln(x+1)^{-2}}=(x+1)^{-2}\)
方程两边乘以积分因子：\((x+1)^{-2}\frac{dy}{dx}-2(x+1)^{-3}y=(x+1)^{3}\cdot(x+1)^{-2}\)
左边为 \(\frac{d}{dx}\left[y\cdot(x+1)^{-2}\right]=(x+1)\)
积分得 \(y\cdot(x+1)^{-2}=\int(x+1)dx=\frac{(x+1)^{2}}{2}+C\)
解得 \(y=(x+1)^{2}\left[\frac{(x+1)^{2}}{2}+C\right]=\frac{(x+1)^{4}}{2}+C(x+1)^{2}\)
代入初始条件 \(x=0\)，\(y=1\)：\(1=\frac{(0+1)^{4}}{2}+C(0+1)^{2}=\frac{1}{2}+C\)
解得 \(C=1-\frac{1}{2}=\frac{1}{2}\)
特解为 \(y=\frac{(x+1)^{4}}{2}+\frac{(x+1)^{2}}{2}=\frac{(x+1)^{2}}{2}\left[(x+1)^{2}+1\right]\)
答案\(y=\frac{(x+1)^{4}}{2}+\frac{(x+1)^{2}}{2}\) 或等价地 \(y=\frac{(x+1)^{2}\left[(x+1)^{2}+1\right]}{2}\)
(9)\(\displaystyle x(1+y^{2})dx-y(1+x^{2})dy=0,\ 当\ x=0\ 时,\ y=2\)
分离变量：\(x(1+y^{2})dx = y(1+x^{2})dy\)，\(\frac{x}{1+x^{2}}dx = \frac{y}{1+y^{2}}dy\)
两边积分：\(\int \frac{x}{1+x^{2}}dx = \int \frac{y}{1+y^{2}}dy\)
计算积分：\(\frac{1}{2}\ln(1+x^{2}) = \frac{1}{2}\ln(1+y^{2}) + C_{1}\)
化简：\(\ln(1+x^{2}) = \ln(1+y^{2}) + 2C_{1}\)，即\(\ln\frac{1+x^{2}}{1+y^{2}} = 2C_{1}\)
设\(C = e^{2C_{1}}\)，得\(\frac{1+x^{2}}{1+y^{2}} = C\)，即\(1+x^{2} = C(1+y^{2})\)
代入初始条件\(x=0, y=2\)：\(1+0 = C(1+4)\)，\(1 = 5C\)，\(C = \frac{1}{5}\)
代入得\(1+x^{2} = \frac{1}{5}(1+y^{2})\)，即\(5(1+x^{2}) = 1+y^{2}\)
整理：\(y^{2} = 5+5x^{2}-1 = 5x^{2}+4\)
由\(y=2>0\)，取\(y = \sqrt{5x^{2}+4}\)
答案\(y = \sqrt{5x^{2}+4}\)
(10)\(\displaystyle y(x)=\int_{0}^{x}y(t)dt+x+1\)
由方程 \( y(x)=\int_{0}^{x}y(t)dt+x+1 \)，令 \( x=0 \) 得 \( y(0)=\int_{0}^{0}y(t)dt+0+1=1 \)

解得 \( C=2 \)
答案\( y(x)=2e^{x}-1 \)
